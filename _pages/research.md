I am driven to understand the genomic architecture behind differential phenotypes between and within species:
	
1\. __What drives differential phenotypes between species with sympatric or parapatric distributions?__
	
		

2\. __How are distinct, complex phenotypes expressed within a species, and what external drivers maintain these polymorphisms?__



3\. __How can modern morphometric and genomic techniques be combined to better understand the current diversity of species?__


I approach these questions through applied ecology and genomic analyses.
